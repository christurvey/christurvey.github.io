Greetings fellow students. We are presenting you with a hip game of hangman. It has been updated by a guy in his mid-40's to try to relate to you. This is a hangman game based on social despair.

How to play

  • Guess the letters to correctly spell a name of something related to social media
  • If you guess correctly, the letter will automatically fill in to the corresponding space(s)
  • To guess the name correctly, you must fill out all the spaces in the name
  • As you guess incorrectly you lose the social love we all need
  • Click the "New Game" Button to start the game
